I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computing at DePaul University. My research focuses on quantum computing and its intersection with machine learning, security, and privacy, with emphasis on quantum machine learning, error modeling and mitigation, and circuit compilation.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from William & Mary, where I was advised by Prof. Qun Li. I also hold a M.S. in Computer Science from Syracuse University and a B.S. in Information Security from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics.
